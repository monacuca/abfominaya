about me/artist statement

I am really honored you made it to my site! Thank you. This site is loosely based off a small part in "A Hundred Years of Solitude" by Gabriel Garcia Marquez that talks about a house with infinite rooms.

I am an interdisciplinary artist and programmer studying many things at Carnegie Mellon University. I grew up in Colombia, during the tendrils of a silent civil war. My art practice is that of an artist in diaspora and as such, I like to find ways to connect to a culture that is both a big and gone part of me.

In here you can find some of my projects, art and programming (and everything in between).

I also have an index for those who find the site easier to naviagate that way.
